Example:
./alsrac.out -s measure -i data/test/mult16_exact.blif -x data/test/mult16_MED_651.25.blif -f 10000000 

-s measure: enable the mode error measurement
-i <exact circuit>: path to exact circuit
-x <approximate circuit>: path to approximate circuit
-f <number of Monte Carlo simulation>: number of Monte Carlo simulation used to obtain the error
